FAQ

For the questions you may or may not have

How do I tell Apescrape what to look for?
With Apescrape you describe the data you want in plain English
Do I have to list every page I want to search?
Not at all! All ApeScrape needs is a list of websites.
What if there are duplicates?
Duplicated entries are detected and removed automagically.
What if the data I'm looking for is split across multiple pages?
Apescrape can aggregate information from multiple webpages into cohesive records.
