FAQ

Questions you might have

How do I tell Apescrape what to look for?
With Apescrape you can simply describe your goals in plain English
Do I have to supply all of the pages?
Not at all! All you have to do it tell us which websites to search, and we will crawl the site to find the information
What if there are duplicates?
Duplicated entries are detected and removed by default
What if the data I'm looking for is spread around?
Apescrape can aggregate information from multiple webpages into cohesive records
